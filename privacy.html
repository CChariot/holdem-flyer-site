Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 18, 2026

Holdem Flyer is designed to help users record poker hands, review decisions, and track poker sessions.

Information you provide

  • Hand histories entered by you
  • Session details entered by you
  • Notes entered by you

How information is used

  • To store and display your poker sessions and hand history
  • To support in-app review and tracking features
  • To improve the app if you contact support about a bug or issue

Data sharing

Holdem Flyer does not sell your personal data. Holdem Flyer does not share personal data with third parties except as reasonably necessary to operate essential app functionality or as required by law.

Data retention and control

You control the information you enter into the app. Depending on app functionality, you may delete app content within the app or remove the app from your device.

Children

Holdem Flyer is not directed to children under 13.
