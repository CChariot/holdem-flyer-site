Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 11, 2026

Holdem Flyer helps you record poker hands, review decisions, and track poker sessions. This policy explains what information the app handles and how.

Data stored on your device

The following stays on your device and is not uploaded unless you explicitly share it (see “Sharing with friends” below):

  • Hand histories you enter
  • Session details you enter (results, stakes, duration, location, notes)

Account data (optional Friends features)

The Friends features are optional. To use them, you sign in with Sign in with Apple or Sign in with Google. When you do, we collect:

  • Your name and email address from the sign-in provider
  • A randomly generated user ID (your “UID”) used to add friends

Sign in with Apple supports Hide My Email, so you may share a private relay address instead of your real one. We use account data only to operate your account and the Friends features. The rest of the app works without an account.

Sharing with friends (requires your consent)

Nothing is uploaded automatically. If — and only if — you tap Share and confirm the consent prompt, your session results (profit, duration, hands played, location, and date) are uploaded to our server and shown to friends you have added, including on the friends leaderboard. Your display name also appears alongside emoji reactions you leave on friends’ results.

You stay in control:

  • Manage Sharing lets you hide any individual session at any time — hiding a previously shared session removes it from our server on your next share.
  • Friends must be added mutually by exchanging UIDs; strangers cannot see your results.

Purchases

The one-time unlock is processed entirely by Apple through In-App Purchase. We never see or store your payment information. Invite codes redeemed in the app are validated on our server without requiring an account.

Data storage

Shared data and account data are stored on Google Cloud servers located in the United States.

What we don’t do

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We do not use your data for advertising and do not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.
  • We do not share personal data with third parties except as reasonably necessary to operate essential app functionality (e.g., cloud hosting) or as required by law.

Data retention and deletion

Data on your device can be deleted in the app or by removing the app. For shared data, you can hide sessions via Manage Sharing (removed from our server on the next share) or contact us to request deletion of your account and all associated server data.

Children

Holdem Flyer is not directed to children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, we will update this page and the effective date.

Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact GitHub Issues.
